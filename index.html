About us

White Rentals is a business-to-business passenger car rental company with years of experience serving corporate clients. We specialize in long-term rental agreements, providing companies with dependable vehicles tailored to their operational needs. Every car in our fleet is selected and maintained to meet the standards that our business partners expect.
